Origin

Hakeem Inventory was born out of a simple need: to provide small and medium-sized businesses with a powerful yet accessible tool to manage their stock. Founded in 2024, we started as a small project to automate manual ledger entries and have since grown into a comprehensive solution.

Development Process

Built with a focus on modern technologies, Hakeem Inventory utilizes Python Flask for a robust backend, MySQL for reliable data storage, and a responsive frontend designed with Bootstrap 5 and custom CSS. Our development process follows agile methodologies, ensuring constant improvements and user-centric features.

  • Scalable Micro-service Inspired Architecture
  • User-Centric UI/UX Design
  • Rigorous Testing & Security Standards

Achievements

Since our launch, we have helped hundreds of businesses optimize their supply chains, reduce waste, and increase profitability. Our system has processed thousands of transactions with 99.9% accuracy.
